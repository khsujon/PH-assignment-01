Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives progress and is key to building a dynamic Bangladesh 2.0.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures that growth is inclusive and long-lasting.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure is the backbone of a thriving nation.

Empowerment

Empowerment of people is at the heart of Bangladesh 2.0.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 is a journey toward innovation, equity, and sustainability, powered by the dreams of its people. By embracing renewable energy, digital transformation, and inclusive growth, we pave the way for a brighter tomorrow. This vision is not just about progress but about creating opportunities for every citizen. A nation free from poverty, inequality, and environmental degradation is within our grasp. Together, we can redefine success with compassion and social responsibility at its heart. Bangladesh can inspire the world as a beacon of hope and possibility."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
